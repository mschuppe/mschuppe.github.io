Marianne Schuppe

Singer Composer Writer

 

My interest in voice draws me into the area between music and language. An area where the grain of words merges with the fluidity of sound.
This suspense is guiding my commitment since the eightees, when I began to delve into the music of Giacinto Scelsi and Morton Feldman and I have pursued it in different ways and collaborations: improvisation, interpretation, reweighing the traditional ingredients of song, developing projects with and for voices, teaching, writing.
In recent years, I have turned more specifically to the borderlands where music passes into poetry. In this vein colline sur livre is since 2022 an ongoing work.
