Most of my works pass through words in their process of becoming. Sometimes words are articulated and come to sound through a voice or an instrument. Sometimes they are transmitted orally, and sometimes they are shared as a text score. Words rarely remain in their initial form. They are spoken, sung, remembered, translated, misheard, or disappear altogether.

My attention is with what words become as they move between listening, writing, speaking, and singing, and as they pass from one person to another.
