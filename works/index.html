works

Most of my work is with and for voices. Voices from different backgrounds, each carrying its own history of speaking, singing, listening, and being heard. I am drawn to the traces that experience leaves in every voice and how they come to sound.

I am interested in how different modes of listening reveal different qualities of the voice. Words invite understanding through a shared vocabulary, while sound may draw attention to breath, rhythm and timbre. Yet words are always sound, and sound is never without meaning.

Words and sound are interweaving layers of the voice. My work unfolds in their interplay and in how listening changes what a voice becomes.
